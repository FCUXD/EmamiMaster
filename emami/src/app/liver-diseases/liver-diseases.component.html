Symptoms of Liver Disease

The Poorvarupa or premonitory symptoms of liver disease are those that occur before the complete manifestation of the disease.

Symptoms of Premonitory Liver Diseases:

In the ancient text CikitsakramaKalpavalli, the following premonitory
symptoms of jaundice or Kamala (a type of liver disease) are mentioned:

thirst
Mild fever

Tastelessness

Indigestion

Decreased digestive capacity or weakened digestive fire
