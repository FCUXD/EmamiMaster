What is Dysmenorrhoea?

It refers to the pain experienced during the menstrual period.

Menstrual cramp or Dysmenorrhoea is the pain experienced during the menstrual period. The intensity of the pain may range from mild to severe; in some women, the severe pain may restrict them from performing daily activities, whereas few women don’t feel any pain.
