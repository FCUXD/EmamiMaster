What is Asthma

Asthma is a chronic breathing disorder in which the airway becomes hyper-responsive on exposure to a particular allergen.

This causes spasmodic narrowing of the airways, which however, can be relieved with treatment. Asthma and allergies usually start in early childhood but asthma attacks can occur in all age groups.

Symptoms of Asthma

The symptoms of asthma are as follows

Sneezing and running nose

Wheezing

Stuffiness in the head and neck part

Rapid breathing with dyspnoea

Severe difficulty in breathing

Disturbed sleep due to breathlessness

Deterioration of consciousness during coughing

Soreness of the throat
