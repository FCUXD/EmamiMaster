What is Insomnia

Insomnia can be considered both as a disorder and a symptom of a health problem.

Insomnia for a long time may lead to various health issues like depression, high blood pressure, diabetes, and other psychiatric disorders.

Symptoms of Insomnia

The symptoms of sleeplessness may vary according to the dosha involved.

- Vata
  • Restless sleep with tossing and turning
  • No sleep at all
  • Waking up between 2 to 4 am
  • Difficulty to go back to sleep till 6 am
- Pita
  • Inability to sleep within 10pm to 2 am
  • High cortisol levels in the body
  • Always irritable
  • Has disturbed dreams of fights
- Kapha
  • Sleep apnea (breathlessness while sleeping)
  • Waking up early in the morning around 6 am
  • Feeling lazy after a full night’s sleep
  • The following are some of the common symptoms of insomnia
  • Roughness and coldness
  • Instability
  • Non-sliminess, motion and formlessness
  • Not feeling fresh in the morning
  • Feeling tired but unable to sleep
  • Lethargy and decreased ability to work
  • Body heaviness and body ache
