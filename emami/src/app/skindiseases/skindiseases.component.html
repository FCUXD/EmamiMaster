Complications of Skin Diseases

Early diagnosis and treatment of Kushthi are essential to avoid the development of complications such as infections
(krimi) in the affected areas. The infections may be the bacterial or fungal type and give rise to the following symptoms:

  • Formation of skin boils
  • Excessive thirst and burning
  • Infections around the nails
  • Infections of the hair follicle
  • Jock itch
  • Vaginal infections
