What is Constipation?

The matter of greatest concern for all Indian parents- Did my child eat food properly? And Is he/she passing stools regularly?

If the answer is yes for both, then they are at ease, because for ages, a good appetite, and regular bowel movements has been considered to be the prime indicator of good health. Almost everyone has constipation once in their life. Insufficient water and/or fiber intake, faulty food habits having certain medications, lack of exercise- all can contribute to constipation.

Symptoms of Constipation

The aggravated vata dosha gives rise to the following preliminary signs of constipation:

Preliminary signs of Constipation
  • Thirst
  • Heaviness in the abdomen (Anaha)
  • Lassitude
  • Debility
  • Burning of food
  • Delayed digestion
  • Loss of appetite (Aruchi)

Complications in Constipation

Constipation is not a very serious problem, but if you are experiencing it for a very long time then the following health issues may set-in:

  • Hernia
  • Rectal prolapse
  • Bowel obstruction
  • Haemorrhoids
  • Irritable bowel syndrome
  • Syncope
