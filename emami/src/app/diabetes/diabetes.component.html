What is Diabetes?

Diabetes is a chronic metabolic disorder in which there is accumulation of excess sugar in blood.

It can be due to:

Decreased Insulin production by the pancreas

Decreased glucose uptake by the body cells

Prevention for Diabetes

The following prevention has been recommended for diabetes

Cereals: Include barley, wheat and bajra
Pulses: Green gram (green moong), chana,pigeon pea (tuvar dal)
Vegetables: All bitter vegetables- bitter gourd (karela), fenugreek (methi), garlic (rason), cluster figs(udumbar)
Fruits: Grossberry (Amala), black plums(jamun), palm sugar, palm dates
Spices: such as peppers, ginger, mustard seeds
