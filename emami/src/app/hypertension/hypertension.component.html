What is Hypertension?

When the blood pressure elevates above 140/90 mmHg, it is considered as hypertension or high blood pressure

If the blood pressure continues to rise and is not controlled, it can lead to serious consequences such as stroke, heart failure, kidney disease, and vision loss.
